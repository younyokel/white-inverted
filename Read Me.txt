How to change the cursor:

1- Right click on the file “Install.inf” and select “Install”
2- Right click on the desktop and select “Personalize”
3- Click on “Change mouse pointers” and then go to the “Pointers” tab
4- Select the “White” scheme in the dropdown and uncheck the box “Enable pointer shadow”
5- Press “OK” and enjoy :)
